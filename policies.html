Booking & Cancellation

  • 24‑hour window: Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance so we can offer the time to another client.
  • Late cancellations / no‑shows: A $25 fee may be requested via Zelle for missed appointments.
  • Arrivals: If you’re running late, please let us know—your session may be shortened to respect the next client.

Mobile Visits (Travel Fee)

Mobile visits are available within the San Jose area. A flat $15 travel fee applies for mobile sessions.

  • Please provide your address during booking if you choose a mobile visit.
  • Ensure safe parking and a clear, quiet space for the massage table.

Payments

  • Accepted: Zelle and cash at time of service.
  • Tips are appreciated but never expected—your comfort is our priority.

Health & Comfort

  • If you’re recovering from surgery or have specific concerns, please share any guidance from your clinician.
  • If you feel unwell (fever or flu‑like symptoms), please reschedule for a later date.
  • Your comfort matters—let us know your preferred pressure and any sensitivities.
