About Lize

Hello, and welcome to Revive by Lize.

I first studied massage therapy in Brazil, where I learned the art of therapeutic touch. Through my work in healthcare here in the U.S.—especially in wound care—I came to understand how essential circulation is for the body’s healing process.

I’ve seen how poor circulation can slow recovery, and how even gentle, mindful touch can help the body restore itself. Whether it’s easing post‑surgical swelling, helping a bruise fade faster, or supporting someone who spends long hours standing or sitting, proper circulation is the foundation of balance and well‑being.

The techniques I use today are gentle, intentional, and guided by your body’s natural rhythm. To me, massage is more than technique—it’s humanized touch, mindful care, and giving your body the chance to recover, reset, and feel whole again.
