About Lize

Hello welcome to Revive by Lize

My journey began in Brazil, where I first studied massage therapy and the art of therapeutic touch. After years working in healthcare here in California, especially with wound care and hospice, I saw firsthand how proper circulation sets the foundation for recovery. When flow improves, everything else follows. That's why Revive by Lize was created, to bridge relaxation and recovery. My work is gentle, intuitive, and always intentional. Every session meets you where you are, physically, emotionally, and energetically, and guides your body back into its natural flow. Because when circulation flows, healing follows.

Note: Revive by Lize is a women-only massage service. Male clients may be accepted by personal referral only.

Services

Tailored to You — Lifestyle by Lize

$120 • ~60 min

A personalized full-body session that adapts to what your body needs that day. No fixed routine, just attentive, intentional touch focused on tension relief, circulation, and balance.

People come to this session for all kinds of reasons: neck and shoulder tension, fatigue from long hours on their feet, tightness from sitting at a desk, or just needing a real reset. The session may also include gentle stretching and range of motion work, making it a great fit for older adults or anyone who benefits from slower, more supportive care.
